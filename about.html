Adam Blevins

Drums

Adam grew up in North Georgia and studied classical percussion at UTC before moving to Birmingham to begin his music career. While in Birmingham, he worked his way up the cover band scene to become one of the top-call rock and country drummers in the area. After twelve years in Birmingham Adam returned to Chattanooga where he began playing with Voodoo Slim. Adam is happy to be back rocking the Scenic City!
