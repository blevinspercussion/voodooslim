Voodoo Slim is a 4-piece rock cover band based in Chattanooga, Tennessee. While their sound is firmly planted in the sounds of artists such as Pearl Jam, Van Halen, Ozzy, and Aerosmith they can easily switch to a song by Lionel Ritchie or Prince and cover them all equally as well.

The group is comprised of seasoned musicians who have all been in bands with genres ranging from rock and metal to dance, country, and jazz. Voodoo Slim has a dynamic stage presence that mixes rock and roll aesthetics with inspiration from the Voodoo traditions of New Orleans.

Voodoo Slim was officially formed in January 2014 and began playing at several popular venues around Dalton GA, Athens TN, and Chattanooga TN. They quickly gained a reputation as one of the hottest party bands in the area and are currently in high demand at bars, venues, and private parties throughout Georgia, Tennessee, and Alabama.

You can catch Voodoo Slim live nearly every weekend performing three to four hours of non-stop hits from the seventies and eighties to today's hottest chart-toppers. The music is fun, and the fun is contagious!

Ian has been singing since he was five years old. He has fronted numerous bands ranging from country and christian rock to metal. His heaviest influences are Elvis Presley, Eddie Vedder, Steve Perry, Layne Staley, and Gene Simmons.
